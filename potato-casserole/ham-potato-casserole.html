Ham, Potato and Broccoli Casserole

This ham, potato and broccoli combination is not only delightful in the eyes but very sumptious and delicious to taste. It is good for a family get together. I make it for my family on every Thanksgiving holiday. It is usually the first to finish on the dining table. Everybody loves it.

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Total Time 45 mins

Servings: 5

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen French fries
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli
  • 1 1/2 cooked, cubed ham
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can milk
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup grated Parmesian cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat over to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Spray a 9x13 inc baking dish with cooking spray. Cover bottom of dish with layer of French fries. Add a layer of broccoli, then sprinkle ham evenly over broccoli. In a small box, mix together soup, milk and mayonnaise. Pour mixture evenly over ingredients in baking dish and sprinkle with cheese.
  3. Bake uncovered in preheated oven for 40 minutes.

Nutrition Facts per serving

Calories: 502

Fat: 31g

Carbs 35g

Protein 23g
